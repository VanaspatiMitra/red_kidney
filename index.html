About the Crop:

Red Kidney Beans (Rajma) - PBR 357 is a high-yielding variety known for its excellent quality and adaptability. It flourishes in well-drained, loamy to sandy loam soils with a pH of 6.0 to 7.5. The crop requires around 600-800 mm of water per acre, which can be supplied through rainfall or irrigation. Typically, PBR 357 produces about 800-1,000 kg of beans per acre. Profit margins can be quite favorable, often ranging from 20-35%, depending on input costs and market prices.

  • Soil Composition: sandy to clay loamy soils with a pH range of 6.0 to 7.5
  • Water need per Acre: 1.97 to 2.62 feet
  • Potential Dangers: Soil Degradation, Pest Infestation, Waterlogging
  • Fertilizers: Diammonium Phosphate, Organic Matter, Potassium, Nitrogen
  • Pesticides: Emamectin benzoate ,Thiamethoxam, Acetamiprid
  • Profit Per Acre: ₹55,400 to ₹1,14,650
